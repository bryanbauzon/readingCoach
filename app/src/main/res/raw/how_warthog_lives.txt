HOW WARTHOG LIVES	"The warthog is one of the strangest creatures in the world it has a mane curving tusks, bumps on its face and a tufted tell like a lion's. Whats more It kneels when it eats. 

When warthogs run, their tails stand straight up in the air. A whole group of them trotting with tails at attention is one of the funniest sights to be seen on the African plains. 

Warthogs make up for their strange looks with courage and family loyalty. When warthogs go to sleep at night, the young ones enter the burrows first. Then the adults back in, so that they face the outside, and their ready to defend their family.
