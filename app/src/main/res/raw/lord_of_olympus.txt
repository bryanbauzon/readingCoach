	"Zeus was the chief of all the Greek gods. The Romans knew him as Jove or as Jupiter. All human affairs were ruled by him. His home end the home of merest to the gods was on Mount Olympus in Greece. He was lord of the Winds and rain and of thunder and lightning.

He is often shown holding lightning and wearing a crown of leaves. 

Zeus knew and saw all. The Greeks  thought of him as a kindly ruler who often showed, pity as we as anger. He was the god the family and of friendship and he protected all of Greece.
