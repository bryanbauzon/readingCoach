THE SINGING BIRD	"Ben has two cats. Their names are Max and Tito. Max is black. Tito is brown. Max has green eyes. Tito
has blue eyes. Max is black with green eyes. Tito is brown with blue eyes. They are fast. They are
beautiful. They are fast and beautiful. Ben also has a bird. Her name is Lilly. Lilly likes to sing. Lilly sings
every day. Sometimes she sings very loud. Max and Tito try to discover who is singing. They look
everywhere! Finally, they see who is singing. It is Lilly! They sit and watch her sing."	Susan Carter
